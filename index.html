Switzerland
Its abundant natural ecosystems like the Alps, wetlands and forests are in close proximity to urban centres and are a particular feature of the Swiss landscape
Iceland
Icelandic nature is unspoilt and mystical with its active volcanoes, tumbling waterfalls, towering mountains and magical lakes.
Australia
Australia is an arid continent; fully one-third of its area is occupied by desert, another third is semidesert, and only in the north, east, southeast, and southwest
